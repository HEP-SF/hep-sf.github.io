Virtual Workshop on New Architectures

WLCG and the HSF are organising a virtual workshop in May covering New Architectures, Portability, and Sustainability.

This is an end-to-end challenge, from low level code portability to workload management and accounting. The goal of the workshop is to exchange best practice and discuss areas of future collaboration and R&D.

We are organising this workshop to optimise for an online event, please register so we can better plan the event.
